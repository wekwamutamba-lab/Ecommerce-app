About Arthaus

Arthaus is a curated online gallery where you can browse and purchase original artworks, paintings, prints, and mixed media pieces directly from Human.her's collection.

How to buy

1

Browse the Gallery and find a piece you love.

2

Click Add to cart. You can add multiple pieces and adjust quantities from your cart.

3

Go to Cart, review your order, then proceed to checkout.

4

Enter your name, email, and delivery address. Your order confirmation is sent instantly.

All pieces ship carefully packaged. Orders over $5000 qualify for free shipping.
